Overview

The Agent was available to purchase for only six days before becoming unobtainable on May 7th, 2021. It could have been bought for 600,000 in-game cash at the central intersection in Rising City. The Agent is unique for being a weaponized car, featuring a pair of missile launchers on the front fenders. They cannot be aimed, instead simply firing in the direction the car is facing. They deal a maximum of 25 damage. They are weaker than other vehicle missiles but still relatively effective for killing players.
